About us


WINDINWILLOWS Maine Coon Cattery was founded in Moscow, Russia in 2009 and is the passion of Elena Cheliadinova and her family. Our cattery is registered with the international professional association TICA (https://www.tica.org/). 

Our journey into breeding Maine Coons was born from a lifelong love affair with felines. Drawn to their wild allure and sweet demeanor, we knew that our lives would forever be intertwined with these magnificent creatures. Over the years, our cattery has become a sanctuary where Maine Coons thrive, their health and happiness our utmost priority.

As breeders, we adhere to the highest standards of excellence, meticulously selecting our breeding stock to ensure the continuation of desirable traits and the elimination of potential health concerns. Through genetic testing and careful observation, we strive to produce Maine Coons of exceptional beauty, temperament, and robustness.

Our dedication to quality has not gone unnoticed, earning us recognition both locally and internationally. From China to the United States, our kittens have found loving homes in every corner of the globe, a testament to the enduring legacy of WindinWillows.
