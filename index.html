Common questions

What is a Maine Coon? The Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated breeds of cat known for its friendly and sociable nature.
How can I adopt a Maine Coon kitten from your cattery? To adopt a Maine Coon kitten from our cattery, you can find our contacts HERE.
Are your Maine Coon cats registered with any cat associations? Yes, all our Maine Coon cats are registered with reputable cat association TICA to ensure their pedigree and quality.
What kind of health checks do your Maine Coons undergo? Our Maine Coons undergo regular health checks, including genetic testing, to ensure they are healthy and free from hereditary diseases. They also get vaccinated before leaving our cattery.
Do you provide any support or guidance for new Maine Coon owners? Yes, we offer support and guidance to new Maine Coon owners to help them care for their new feline family member and ensure a smooth transition.
